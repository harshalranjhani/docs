The `support` command is used to support Genie. This opens a payment page where you can contribute to the development and maintenance of Genie.

## Usage

```bash
genie support
```

Once your payment is processed, you will receive a confirmation email. Your support is greatly appreciated and helps us continue to improve Genie for all users.

There are no additional arguments required for this command.
